
Fontes e bases técnicas:

1. Machining Data Handbook (Metcut Research)
   - Índices de usinabilidade, vida útil e parâmetros de corte.

2. ISO 13399 – Representação digital de ferramentas de corte
   - https://www.iso.org/standard/59875.html

3. Sandvik Coromant Tooling Data
   - https://www.sandvik.coromant.com

4. Ontologias para manufatura:
   - MASON Ontology (NIST): https://www.nist.gov/services-resources/software/mason
   - OntoSTEP: https://github.com/nist/OntoSTEP
   - OntoCAPE (engenharia de processo): http://www.ontocape.org

5. Owlready2 – Ontologia e raciocínio em Python:
   - https://owlready2.readthedocs.io

6. SWRL (Semantic Web Rule Language):
   - https://www.w3.org/Submission/SWRL/

GitHub com exemplos práticos:
- https://github.com/pchampin/owlready2
- https://github.com/industrial-ontology/ (diversos padrões OWL para indústria)
